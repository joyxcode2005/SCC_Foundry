# FOUNDRY 

**Leaderboard & Task Assignment Platform**

Welcome to the official repository for FOUNDRY. This platform is designed to manage projects, assign tasks fairly, and gamify contributions through a public leaderboard for our tech society.

## Platform Features
* **Project Board:** All active projects are listed on a centralized projects page.
* **Categorized Sub-Tasks:** Projects are broken down into specific, manageable sub-tasks. Each task is tagged with descriptive labels based on the required skill set (e.g., `TECH`, `VIDEO`, `PHOTO`, `CONTENT`).
* **Public Leaderboard:** A transparent, real-time leaderboard allows all members to track their current standings and points.
* **Google Drive Integration:** For non-code tasks (Video, Photo, Content, etc.), members can easily submit their deliverables to designated, secure Google Drive project folders for moderator review.

## How It Works (The Workflow)
1. **Discover Tasks:** Members can browse the projects page and view available sub-tasks.
2. **Show Interest:** Members click the **"Interested"** button on a task they wish to claim. 
3. **Task Assignment:** Tasks are assigned strictly on a **First-Come, First-Served (FCFS)** basis. 
4. **Submission:** Once the work is complete, members submit their pull requests (for code) or upload their files to the shared Google Drive (for media/content).
5. **Review & Merge:** Moderators review the submitted work. 
6. **Scoring:** Upon successful approval and merging of the task by a moderator, the member is automatically awarded **10 points**.

## Rules & Guidelines
To ensure fairness and maintain a healthy workflow, all members must adhere to the following rules:

* **Standardized Scoring:** Every successfully completed and merged task is worth exactly **10 points**.
Weekly Limits: To prevent burnout and ensure everyone gives 100 percent on the work they do, members are capped at taking a maximum of 2 to 3 tasks per week.
* **Task Capacity limits:** Each individual task has a maximum limit on how many members can apply for it. Once the assignment cap is reached, the task will no longer accept new "Interested" requests.
* **Flexible Deadlines:** Deadlines are project-dependent. A task may or may not have a strict deadline based on the specific needs of that project.
* **End-of-Season Rewards:** Hard work pays off! The top **3 to 5 members** on the leaderboard with the highest points will be officially recognized and awarded.
